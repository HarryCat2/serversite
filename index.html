Sherlock - Verify Before You Share

politics.sherlock.com | ★ 4.7 (2.6k ratings)

Verify political or factual content on social media platforms before reposting it.

Overview

Sherlock is the ultimate tool for users seeking to ensure their shared content is accurate, responsible, and credible. This lightweight extension integrates seamlessly with popular social media platforms like Twitter, Facebook, and Reddit. Its algorithm scans posts for political and factual claims, cross-references them with verified databases, and issues a warning if content contains possible misinformation.

Designed with user privacy in mind, Sherlock does not store any personal browsing data. It works entirely in your browser, ensuring a secure and reliable fact-checking experience without compromising speed or performance.

Features

  • Real-time content verification for political and factual claims.
  • Seamless integration with Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, and more.
  • Detailed explanations with sources for flagged content.
  • Lightweight design with no impact on browsing speed.

Privacy Practices

Sherlock prioritizes user privacy above all else. The extension processes all data locally in your browser and does not send any information to external servers. Your browsing history, social media activity, and personal data are never collected, stored, or shared.

FAQs

How does Sherlock verify information?

Sherlock uses a combination of AI-driven algorithms and trusted fact-checking databases to analyze claims.

Does Sherlock work on all social media platforms?

Currently, Sherlock supports Twitter, Facebook, and Reddit, with plans to expand to more platforms in future updates.

Can I customize the alerts?

Yes! Users can adjust alert sensitivity in the settings menu to match their preferences.
